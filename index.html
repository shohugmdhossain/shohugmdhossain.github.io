EARTH & PLANETARY SCIENCE • GEOAI

Turning satellite data into actionable insights for Earth and planetary science

I’m a Ph.D. researcher specializing in remote sensing, satellite imagery, and geospatial AI. My work combines machine learning and high-performance computing to study landscapes on Earth and Mars. I am passionate about advancing planetary exploration and Earth sustainability through remote sensing. I have hands-on experience with multispectral and hyperspectral data analysis, geospatial modeling, and large-scale satellite data processing. Beyond the technical side, I focus on translating complex datasets into insights that drive climate action, urban planning, and planetary science.
