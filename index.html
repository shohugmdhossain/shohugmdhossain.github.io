Exploring Earth and Beyond

I’m a Ph.D. researcher exploring how GeoAI and remote sensing can reveal hidden stories within planetary and environmental data. My work bridges machine learning and geoscience to map surface evolution across both Earth and Mars.

With a passion for discovery and data-driven analysis, I aim to build intelligent systems that help us better understand our planet, its analogs, and the forces shaping their landscapes.
