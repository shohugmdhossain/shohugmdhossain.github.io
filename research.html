Research Overview

My research lies at the intersection of planetary geomorphology, remote sensing, and artificial intelligence. I develop data-driven frameworks that apply deep learning and physics-informed modeling to understand surface evolution on Earth and Mars. By integrating multisensor satellite data with high-performance computing, my work aims to advance reproducible GeoAI methods for terrain interpretation, environmental monitoring, and planetary exploration.

Research Themes

Planetary Geomorphology

Study of Martian and terrestrial landforms through AI-based terrain analysis, quantifying landscape evolution and morphodynamics.

GeoAI & Deep Learning

Developing neural networks for automated landform detection, segmentation, and geomorphic classification across planetary datasets.

Remote Sensing Integration

Fusing radar, optical, and hyperspectral imagery to analyze surface processes, hydrology, and geological change across scales.

Physics-Informed Modeling

Designing interpretable AI models grounded in geophysical constraints to promote trustworthy and reproducible GeoAI.

Present Projects

Automated Mound Detection on Mars
University of Louisiana at Lafayette · 2025–Present

Developing deep-learning frameworks for automatic detection and classification of mound-like geomorphologies using CTX and HiRISE imagery.

  • Implemented Faster R-CNN and Detectron2 models on LONI HPC clusters for large-scale planetary mapping.
  • Generated precision–recall metrics and interpretive overlays for geomorphic accuracy assessment.
  • Optimized dataset registration and augmentation to improve regional consistency and recall.

Spectral Unmixing of CRISM Imagery Using Autoencoders
Independent Project · 2025

Exploring nonlinear unmixing of CRISM hyperspectral data to derive mineralogical distributions and surface alteration signatures.

  • Developed deep autoencoder architectures for endmember extraction and abundance estimation.
  • Benchmarked deep-learning outcomes against classical linear unmixing models.
  • Integrated results with terrain data for context-driven mineralogical interpretation.
