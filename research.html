Research in GeoAI & Planetary Science

Artificial Intelligence for Earth Observation and Planetary Exploration

Research Overview

My work bridges machine learning and geoscience to analyze planetary surfaces and environmental processes. I develop interpretable AI models using multi-sensor satellite data, physics-informed learning, and geospatial analytics.

Current Projects (2024–2025)

Mars Mound Detection Using Deep Learning

Planetary Geomorphology · Ongoing

Developing deep-learning frameworks (Faster R-CNN, Mask R-CNN) on CTX imagery to automatically detect mound-like features on Mars. Trained on LONI A100 GPUs, the project enhances automated crater analysis and supports geomorphological mapping.

Detectron2 PyTorch Planetary AI GPU Computing

CRISM Spectral Mineral Mapping

JHU/APL Collaboration

Applying autoencoder-based unmixing on CRISM hyperspectral data to map alteration minerals on Mars. The work supports mineralogical interpretation of aqueous environments through spectral feature learning and radiative-transfer modeling.

CRISM Spectral Python Deep Unmixing

AI-Assisted Label Validation for Street Imagery

Urban Accessibility · Ongoing

Building automated label-auditing pipelines using LLM-assisted verification and computer vision models (YOLOv8, Faster R-CNN) for sidewalk element detection. Focuses on increasing data reliability and explainability in AI-driven urban datasets.

YOLOv8 LLM Vision Python
Past Projects (Before 2024)

Wetland Hydrodynamics & Vegetation Monitoring

Ecosystem Dynamics · 2023

Integrated Sentinel-1 SAR and MODIS optical time series to model floodplain hydrology and vegetation stress. Demonstrated radar–optical synergy for detecting water-level variation and vegetation phenology.

SAR MODIS Earth Observation

Land-Cover Change Detection in Bangladesh

Remote Sensing · 2022

Designed a Random Forest and XGBoost classification system using Landsat-8 and Sentinel-2 imagery to detect vegetation loss and urban expansion. Achieved 92% accuracy through spectral–temporal feature fusion.

Landsat Sentinel-2 XGBoost

Urban Heat Island Modeling

Climate Analytics · 2021

Modeled surface temperature variability using Landsat TIR and MODIS emissivity data. Quantified correlations between urban morphology and heat anomalies across megacities in South Asia.
