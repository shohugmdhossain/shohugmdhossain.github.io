Research Overview

My research integrates Geospatial Artificial Intelligence (GeoAI), remote sensing, and planetary science to analyze multisensor Earth and planetary data. I focus on interpretable machine learning models, large-scale data processing, and physics-informed frameworks to enhance environmental and planetary understanding.

Current & Ongoing Projects

Mars Mound Detection Using Deep Learning

Planetary Geomorphology · 2024–Present

Applying Faster R-CNN and Mask R-CNN frameworks to detect mound-like geomorphologic features in CTX imagery from Mars. Leveraging LONI A100 GPU clusters for large-scale geological surface mapping.

Faster R-CNN Detectron2 COCO JSON LONI GPU

CRISM Spectral Mineral Mapping

Planetary Spectroscopy · 2024–Present

Developing deep unmixing networks for CRISM hyperspectral imagery to characterize surface mineral diversity and identify hydrated minerals. The study aims to infer past aqueous environments in Martian terrains.

CRISM Autoencoders Spectral Python Deep Learning

Urban Accessibility Mapping with AI

Computer Vision · 2024

Constructing a vision-based workflow to identify sidewalk elements—such as curb ramps and crosswalks— from urban imagery using Faster R-CNN and YOLOv8. Enhanced validation accuracy through LLM-assisted label review and systematic dataset correction.

YOLOv8 Python Faster R-CNN LLM QA

Remote Sensing · 2023
