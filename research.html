Research Overview

My research lies at the intersection of Geospatial Artificial Intelligence (GeoAI), remote sensing, and planetary science. I develop interpretable deep-learning models that integrate physics-based reasoning with data-driven discovery to improve our understanding of landscapes on Earth and Mars.

Current Projects (2024–2025)

Mars Mound Detection Using Deep Learning

Developing Faster R-CNN and Mask R-CNN models to detect mound-like structures on Mars from CTX imagery. The project leverages LONI A100 GPU computing and Detectron2 pipelines to improve crater morphology mapping.

Keywords: Detectron2 · PyTorch · GPU · Planetary AI

CRISM Spectral Mineralogy Mapping

Applying deep spectral unmixing on CRISM hyperspectral data in collaboration with Johns Hopkins APL. The study identifies alteration minerals on Martian surfaces and reconstructs past aqueous environments.

Keywords: CRISM · Spectral Python · Deep Unmixing · JHU/APL

Urban Accessibility Mapping with AI

Building computer vision workflows using YOLOv8 and Faster R-CNN for automated detection of curb ramps, crosswalks, and traffic-control devices from street imagery. The system enhances sidewalk inventory datasets.

Keywords: YOLOv8 · Computer Vision · Accessibility · Deep Learning

AI Label Validation for Remote Sensing

Designing automated pipelines for label correction and validation quality assessment in large geospatial datasets using LLM-assisted review and confusion-matrix analytics.

Keywords: Data Curation · Label QA · Confusion Matrix · LLM Vision

Past Projects (Before 2024)

Wetland Hydrodynamics & Vegetation Monitoring

Combined Sentinel-1 SAR and MODIS time-series imagery to model water dynamics and vegetation stress across coastal wetlands, demonstrating radar–optical synergy for monitoring ecosystem resilience.

Keywords: Sentinel-1 · MODIS · Time Series · Wetland Dynamics

Land-Cover Change Detection in Bangladesh

Developed Random Forest and XGBoost classifiers integrating Landsat 8 and Sentinel-2 imagery to monitor vegetation loss and urban expansion with over 90% accuracy.

Keywords: Landsat 8 · Sentinel-2 · XGBoost · Land-Use Change

Urban Heat Island Modeling

Analyzed urban surface temperatures using Landsat TIR and MODIS emissivity to explore correlations between surface reflectance and thermal patterns.

Keywords: MODIS · TIR · Urban Climate · Remote Sensing
