Projects

Research

Planetary GeoAI — Mars Mounds (CTX)

Object detection with Faster/Mask R‑CNN to identify mound features in Mars CTX imagery. COCO pipelines, Detectron2, and GPU training on LONI A100. Ongoing evaluation to ensure reliable AP/AR.

Urban GeoAI — Sidewalk Inventory

Automated label auditing and per-feature confusion matrices for sidewalk elements; improved F1 by correcting mislabeled validation samples; feature-specific insights for safer streets.
