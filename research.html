Research Overview

My research integrates remote sensing, machine learning, and planetary science to investigate geomorphological and environmental processes on Earth and Mars. I emphasize interpretable AI models, physics-based reasoning, and high-performance computing for large-scale geospatial analysis.

Current Projects (2024–2025)

Project Description Methods / Tools
Mars Mound Detection Automated identification of mound-like structures on Mars using Faster R-CNN and Mask R-CNN models trained on CTX imagery via LONI A100 GPUs, improving crater morphology mapping. Detectron2 · PyTorch · Planetary AI
CRISM Spectral Mapping Deep spectral-unmixing models for CRISM hyperspectral data in collaboration with JHU/APL, revealing aqueous alteration minerals across Martian terrains. Autoencoder · Spectral Python · CRISM
Urban Accessibility AI Using YOLOv8 and Faster R-CNN for automatic detection of sidewalk elements (curb ramps, crosswalks, traffic controls) to support accessibility and urban analytics. YOLOv8 · Computer Vision · LLM Validation

Past Projects (Before 2024)

Project Description Methods / Tools
Wetland Hydrodynamics Modeled water-level dynamics and vegetation stress using Sentinel-1 SAR and MODIS optical series, demonstrating radar–optical synergy for coastal wetland monitoring. Sentinel-1 · MODIS · Google Earth Engine
Land-Cover Change Detection Mapped urban expansion and vegetation loss in Bangladesh using Landsat 8 + Sentinel-2 imagery with XGBoost and Random Forest classifiers. Landsat 8 · Sentinel-2 · XGBoost
Urban Heat Island Modeling Analyzed surface-temperature variability using Landsat TIR and MODIS emissivity to quantify correlations between land-cover patterns and heat anomalies. MODIS · Landsat TIR · Python
