Research Overview

My research explores the integration of artificial intelligence, geospatial analytics, and remote sensing to understand and model the physical processes shaping planetary and terrestrial environments. Below are the main themes that guide my current and ongoing work.

Planetary GeoAI and Martian Surface Analysis

My planetary research investigates how deep learning and computer vision can reveal geomorphic patterns and sedimentary processes on Mars. I develop GeoAI frameworks to detect and classify surface mounds, craters, and related landforms using high-resolution orbital imagery. These approaches support the reconstruction of past hydrological and climatic conditions and contribute to the broader understanding of planetary evolution and habitability.

The work bridges geoscience and machine learning by combining quantitative surface mapping with data-driven geomorphological interpretation, helping advance autonomous planetary exploration and comparative planetology.

Earth Observation and Urban Accessibility

On Earth, my research focuses on data-driven urban analytics and environmental monitoring. I apply remote sensing and AI methods to evaluate walkability, accessibility, and infrastructure resilience through automated mapping of sidewalks, curb ramps, crosswalks, and surface conditions. This work links geospatial data with human-centered design, supporting inclusive and sustainable urban development.

By integrating multi-source imagery and spatial validation frameworks, I aim to produce accurate, scalable datasets that inform city planning, transportation equity, and climate adaptation strategies.

Physics-Informed Machine Learning and Multi-Sensor Fusion

A central direction of my research is embedding physical reasoning into AI models for Earth and planetary sciences. I explore physics-informed neural networks and multi-sensor fusion of optical, radar, and thermal observations to achieve robust and interpretable predictions of surface and subsurface phenomena.

This work contributes to the broader effort to create trustworthy GeoAI systems that integrate domain knowledge with data-driven learning, enabling more reliable analysis of environmental and planetary processes at scale.
