Research Overview

My research explores how GeoAI, remote sensing, and planetary data analytics reveal geomorphic processes shaping Earth and Mars. I build interpretable ML workflows—leveraging multispectral and radar imagery—to translate pixels into geophysical insight.

Research Themes

Planetary Science & GeoAI

Detecting mound-like landforms and spectral signatures to reconstruct Martian surface history with interpretable ML.

Earth & Environmental Analytics

Urban accessibility and wetland dynamics via SAR/optical fusion, robust validation, and scalable model training.

Geospatial Modeling & Land Dynamics

Long-term land-cover change, thermal patterns, and landscape modeling using ensemble learning approaches.
