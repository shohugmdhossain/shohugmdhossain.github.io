Research Overview

My research bridges Geospatial Artificial Intelligence (GeoAI), remote sensing, and planetary science. I focus on developing interpretable deep learning and machine learning frameworks that extract meaningful insights from multisensor Earth and planetary imagery. My projects combine physics-informed modeling, large-scale satellite data processing, and computer vision for advancing environmental and planetary observation.

Current & Ongoing Projects

Mars Mound Detection Using Deep Learning

Planetary Geomorphology · 2024–Present
Applying Faster R-CNN and Mask R-CNN models to detect and classify mound-like geological structures on the Martian surface using CTX imagery. The project leverages LONI A100 GPU clusters to improve detection accuracy and automate large-scale geomorphic mapping across cratered terrains.
Faster R-CNN Detectron2 COCO JSON GPU

CRISM Spectral Mineral Mapping

Planetary Spectroscopy · 2024–Present
Mapping surface mineral composition and alteration signatures on Mars using CRISM hyperspectral data. Developing autoencoder-based spectral unmixing models to identify hydrated minerals and compositional diversity associated with aqueous processes.
CRISM Autoencoders SPy Deep Unmixing

Urban Accessibility Mapping with AI

Computer Vision · 2024
Building a vision-based pipeline to detect and validate sidewalk elements such as curb ramps, crosswalks, and traffic controls. Integrating LLM-assisted label auditing to improve dataset consistency and per-feature detection accuracy for accessibility mapping.
YOLOv8 Faster R-CNN LLM QA Python

Past Research Projects

Wetland Hydrodynamics & Vegetation Mapping

Remote Sensing · 2023
Integrated Sentinel-1 SAR and MODIS NDVI time-series to monitor hydrologic variability and vegetation response in coastal wetlands. Demonstrated radar-optical synergy for analyzing floodplain resilience during seasonal monsoon cycles.
Sentinel-1 MODIS NDVI Google Earth Engine

Land-Cover Change Detection in Bangladesh

Geospatial ML · 2022
Designed a Random Forest and XGBoost ensemble to analyze land-use transitions using Landsat 8 and Sentinel-2 imagery. Quantified urban expansion and vegetation loss between 2013–2022, providing geospatial insights for sustainable planning.
Sentinel-2 Landsat 8 XGBoost Random Forest

Urban Heat Island Modeling

Urban Climate · 2021
Investigated urban thermal anomalies using Landsat TIR and MODIS emissivity data to examine surface temperature variability across major South Asian cities. Enhanced urban heat island modeling through emissivity correction techniques.
