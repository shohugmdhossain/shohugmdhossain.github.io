About Me

Md Shohug Hossain is a Ph.D. Researcher specializing in GeoAI, remote sensing, and planetary science.

Ph.D. Researcher, Department of Earth and Energy Sciences · University of Louisiana at Lafayette, USA

My work bridges artificial intelligence with Earth and planetary data analysis to decode geomorphic and environmental processes. I build deep-learning frameworks for automatic terrain analysis and landform mapping using satellite imagery of Earth and Mars. My aim is to develop reproducible GeoAI workflows that contribute to planetary exploration, climate studies, and sustainable Earth observation.
