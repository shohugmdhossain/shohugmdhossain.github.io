About Me

Md Shohug Hossain is a Ph.D. Researcher specializing in GeoAI, remote sensing, and planetary science.

Ph.D. Researcher, Department of Earth and Energy Sciences · University of Louisiana at Lafayette, USA

My work bridges machine learning with Earth and planetary data analysis to understand geomorphic and environmental processes on Earth and Mars. I focus on developing reproducible and interpretable GeoAI frameworks for landform classification, surface mapping, and climate-related applications.
