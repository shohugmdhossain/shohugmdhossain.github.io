About Md Shohug Hossain

Ph.D. Researcher · GeoAI · Remote Sensing · Planetary Science

Professional Overview

Md Shohug Hossain is a Ph.D. researcher at the University of Louisiana at Lafayette specializing in GeoAI and planetary remote sensing. He bridges machine learning, geology, and satellite data science to analyze Earth and Martian landscapes. His research focuses on deep learning models for geomorphological mapping and planetary surface evolution.

He is motivated by the idea that artificial intelligence can extend human observation beyond Earth, transforming planetary data into scientific insight for climate change and space exploration.

Areas of Expertise

GeoAI & Deep Learning

Designing neural networks for geospatial feature recognition and spatiotemporal analysis across planetary datasets.

Remote Sensing & GIS

Processing multispectral satellite imagery to analyze landform changes and surface composition patterns.

HPC & Cloud Computing

Deploying AI pipelines on GPU clusters and cloud environments for large-scale planetary data processing.
