About Me

I am Md Shohug Hossain, a Ph.D. researcher at the University of Louisiana at Lafayette specializing in GeoAI, Remote Sensing, and Planetary Science. My research focuses on deep learning models for analyzing planetary surfaces and terrestrial landscapes using satellite data and HPC systems.
