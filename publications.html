Automated Mound Detection on Mars Using Deep Learning

Md Shohug Hossain, ABC · Planetary Science Journal (Under Review, 2025)

Introduces a GeoAI framework leveraging Faster R-CNN for large-scale detection of mound-like geomorphologies across Martian CTX imagery. Evaluated across multi-resolution datasets with precision–recall benchmarking.

Md Shohug Hossain, ABC · Remote Sensing of Environment (In Preparation, 2025)

Proposes a nonlinear deep unmixing model for CRISM hyperspectral imagery to identify surface mineralogy and infer aqueous alteration histories across Martian terrains.
