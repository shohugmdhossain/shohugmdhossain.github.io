Publications

A collection of research papers and technical works in GeoAI, remote sensing, and planetary science. This section highlights published and in-review contributions focused on machine learning, geospatial analytics, and Earth observation.

Efficient Object Detection on Martian Surfaces Using Deep Learning

2025 · Under Review, Planetary & Space Science

Developed a Faster R-CNN-based approach to detect mound-like geomorphic structures across Mars CTX imagery, integrating HPC training on LONI clusters.

AI-Driven Wetland Classification from Multispectral Imagery

2023 · Remote Sensing Applications: Society & Environment

Presented a hybrid ensemble integrating Sentinel-1 SAR and MODIS data for wetland dynamics analysis in deltaic ecosystems under monsoon influence.

Behavioral Recommendation Modeling Using Implicit Interaction Data

2021 · Proceedings of IEEE ICICT

Designed a recommender system based on mouse-movement analytics to predict learner preferences, enhancing adaptive digital learning systems.

Land-Cover Change Detection Using Machine Learning Ensembles

2020 · Journal of Environmental Monitoring & Assessment

Applied Random Forest and XGBoost ensemble classifiers on Landsat and Sentinel datasets to quantify land-use transitions and urbanization trends.
