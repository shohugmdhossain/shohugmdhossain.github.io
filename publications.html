Publications & Research Outputs

My research bridges GeoAI, planetary geomorphology, and remote sensing. These works focus on automating terrain interpretation through machine learning and data-driven analytics across Earth and Mars.

This collection includes peer-reviewed papers, conference proceedings, and datasets advancing planetary surface understanding through AI and remote-sensing integration.

Peer-Reviewed Journal Articles

Automated Mound Detection on Mars Using Deep Learning
Md Shohug Hossain, ABC · Planetary Science Journal (Under Review, 2025)

Introduces a GeoAI framework leveraging Faster R-CNN for large-scale detection of mound-like geomorphologies across Martian CTX imagery, benchmarked using precision–recall evaluation and multi-resolution validation.

Spectral Unmixing of CRISM Imagery Using Autoencoder Networks
Md Shohug Hossain, ABC · Remote Sensing of Environment (In Preparation, 2025)

Proposes a nonlinear deep unmixing framework for CRISM hyperspectral imagery to identify mineralogical variations and infer aqueous alteration histories across Martian terrains.
